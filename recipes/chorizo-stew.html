Chorizo-stew

Description

A simple four ingredient chorizo and butter bean stew

Cooks in just 20 minutes, eat with bread on the side for a tasty, quick, rustic stew

Ingredients

  • 200 g cooking chorizo
  • 2x400 g cans chopped tomato
  • 2x400 g cans drained butter beans
  • 1 tub of fresh pesto

Steps

  1. Slice the chorizo and tip into a large saucepan over a medium heat
  2. Fry gently for 5 mins or until starting to turn dark brown
  3. Add the tomatoes and butter beans, bring to the boil, then simmer for 10 mins
  4. Swirl through the pesto, season lightly and ladle into four bowls.
